The auto-fit keyword is like saying, "fit as many columns as you can in this track based on the applied rules." Columns will reflow based on screen width, so while you may have 5 columns on a wide screen, you may only have 2 on a narrow screen. Minmax() function defines an upper and lower limit for your grid track size. In this example we're saying "columns in this track can't be smaller than 200px and they can be as large as 1 fraction unit(the rest of the space)."
